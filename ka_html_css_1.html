Travel to beautiful Chongqing!

Chongqing is one of China's most popular destinations for foreign tourists. Known as China's 'Cyberpunk City', Chongqing is famous for its skyscrapers, stairs, immense verticality, hotpot, and stairs!

Essential Travel Tips

Important: Bring comfortable walking shoes!

  1. Pack light—you'll be carrying your luggage up stairs
  2. Stay hydrated—all those stairs will make you thirsty
  3. Try the spicy hotpot—you'll need the energy for climbing
  4. Embrace the stairs—they're part of the authentic Chongqing experience!
